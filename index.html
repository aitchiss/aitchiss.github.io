About

I am a Software Developer based in Edinburgh, currently working for Nucleus Financial, specialising in React development.

I am also the creator and maintainer of Up Your A11y, a resource for web developers to find useful information on how to make web apps more accessible, particularly focusing on Single Page Applications made in React.

I also blog regularly on DEV, and contribute to Open Source projects, particularly with the aim of auditing and solving accessibility issues.
